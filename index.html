The Namikian Project

The Namikian Project blurred the lines between fashion and fantasy, offering wearers a chance to express their love for Dragon Ball while turning heads with unique, Namikian-inspired footwear. It showcased the potential of fashion to tell a story and transport fans into the enchanting world of Dragon Ball, leaving an indelible mark on the fashion industry as a symbol of creative ingenuity and cultural fusion.

The Blue Speckter

"The Blue and the Ube" marked an extraordinary collaboration that bridged the realms of fashion and culinary art. This project brought a prominent shoe designer together with the vibrant colors and flavors of blueberries and ube, a purple yam. The goal was to create a line of shoes that not only embodied the visual appeal of these ingredients but also celebrated their cultural significance.
